Featured Case Study

Technology Acquisition Assessment: Data-Intensive Infrastructure Platform

A large scientific infrastructure programme needed to move extreme volumes of data — estimates in the tens of terabits per second and hundreds of petabytes per year. The challenge wasn't "buy a data centre." It was designing an acquisition strategy for a capability that doesn't exist locally as an off-the-shelf product.

  1. Identified the future-critical capability and the system-level requirements
  2. Assessed technology readiness vs market readiness and where development was required
  3. Evaluated organisational absorptive capacity — can the client actually adopt and run it?
  4. Assessed partner and consortium suitability for delivery at scale
  5. Produced a decision framework showing conditions were favourable to proceed

Outcome: A defensible acquisition roadmap — what to build, what to buy, who to partner with, and where the main risks sit before procurement begins.
